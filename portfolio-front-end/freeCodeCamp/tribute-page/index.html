Silvio Santos

The best Brazilian presenter

Here's a time line of Senor Abravanel:

  • 1930 - Born in Lapa, Rio de Janeiro.
  • 1948 - He served the Brazilian Army at the Parachute School, in the Deodoro neighborhood, in the west zone of Rio de Janeiro, where he excelled with good jumps.
  • 1962 - He debuted his first tv show called "Vamos Brincar de Forca", which was aired on TV Paulista at night, having achieved immense success with it.
  • 1964 - He began to run his program on Sundays from 12 noon to 2:00 p.m.
  • 1970 - The TV station, that has worked, begin to reform the quality standard of programming. And that made their program he did devalued.
  • 1972 - Left the TV station.
  • 1976 - He began to make programs in Rede Tupi (another TV station).
  • 1977 - His wife Maria Aparecida Vieira died due to cancer, with his first daughter, Silvia, who was adopted by the couple.
  • 1978 - Married Íris Pássaro.
  • 1980 - After the bankruptcy of the Tupi Network in 1980, the Silvio Santos Program in São Paulo was transferred to Rede Record. Silvio came to own 50% of the station of businessman Paulo Machado de Carvalho. However, Silvio planned to have a national television network, produce a full schedule and use the channel for his sweepstakes and promotions.
  • 1981 - Silvio Santos obtained the license to operate channel 4 of São Paulo, which became the TVS of the city of São Paulo. From the broadcasters of Rio and São Paulo, the SBT embryo emerged. The network expanded rapidly through affiliations, but the Silvio Santos Program continued to be transmitted simultaneously by Record, especially to reach the interior of São Paulo. The SBT brand came into use throughout the network in the late 1980s.
  • 1989 - Silvio decided to run for president, but without success.
